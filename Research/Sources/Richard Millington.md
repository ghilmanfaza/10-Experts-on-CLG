# Richard Millington

---

One of the earliest and most data-driven community strategist, best known for bringing structure, measurement, and behavioral science into community building. He's also the founder of **[FeverBee](https://www.feverbee.com/)**

Richard is very different from other experts, he's analytical, experimental, and metric-focused. He expresses that a successful community changes what people do, not just what they feel. If behavior doesn't change, then the community isn't working.
