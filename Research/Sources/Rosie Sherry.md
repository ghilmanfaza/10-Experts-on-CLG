# Rosie Sherry

---

An indie community builder, founder of **[Ministryoftesting](https://www.ministryoftesting.com/)** and **[Rosieland](https://rosie.land/)**, and educator known for her practical and straight forward approach to building communities.

Her thinking is practical, iterative, and human. She emphasize on making thing simple, don't overthink strategy, start small and learn from experiences. Because she sees community as journey. There's no "perfect start", community evolves over time and member's role changes as it grows.
