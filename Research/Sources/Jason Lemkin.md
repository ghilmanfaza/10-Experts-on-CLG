# Jason Lemkin

---

One of the most influential voice in B2B SaaS growth. A founder-turned-investor and the man behind **[SaaStr](https://www.saastr.com/)**, the largest global community for SaaS founders, executives, and operators. His advice comes from operator experience, not theory.

His framework isn't "community-first", but "revenue-first and execution-heavy". Emphasize on knowing "what works" first before upscaling. To be able to achieve that, he pushes founders to aim as big as possible and play the "high risk high reward" game. Scale what works, and left what don't.
